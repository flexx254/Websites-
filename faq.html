Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Learn more about my approach to website design and development in Kenya, including how I help you get started for free and support you long-term through simple, affordable plans.

1. Do you really build websites for free?

Yes. I offer free website design and development to help individuals, businesses, and startups in Kenya establish a strong online presence — without the high upfront cost.

2. What do I get with the free website?

You get a professionally designed, fast-loading, mobile-friendly website tailored to your brand and goals.

3. How can I get started?

Visit the home page and reach out. I’ll guide you through the steps.

4. Why is it free?

To help you launch without budget stress. As you grow, you can upgrade to a simple subscription.

5. What does the subscription plan include?

It covers hosting, updates, SEO, backups, and personalized support.

6. Will I need to buy hosting or pay for a domain?

No. I handle all technical aspects like hosting and domain setup for you.

7. Can I update my website myself?

Yes, but many prefer I handle updates as part of their plan.

8. Is SEO part of the service?

Yes, basic SEO is included. Ongoing SEO is part of the monthly plan.

9. What types of businesses do you work with?

Startups, local shops, professionals, NGOs, and more across Kenya.

10. What makes your service different?

Simplicity, affordability, and genuine long-term support set me apart.

11. How long does it take to build my site?

Usually within 3 to 7 days, depending on your content readiness.

12. Can I use my own domain name?

Yes. I can connect your existing domain or help you get a new one.

13. Do you design logos or branding?

Basic branding assistance is available. Full branding is optional at a low cost.

14. Can I sell products on the website?

Yes. E-commerce features can be added upon request.

15. How do I contact you for support?

Through the contact form on the homepage or WhatsApp link provided.
